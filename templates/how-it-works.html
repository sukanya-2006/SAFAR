How Safar works

Simple steps to your next adventure

1️⃣

Log In or Sign Up

Create your account to save your chat history and travel plans, and access them anytime, anywhere.

2️⃣

Pick a Destination

Just let Safar know where you're headed. Whether it's a specific city or a general region, the AI gets to work instantly.

3️⃣

Tell Us Your Vibe

Are you a budget backpacker, a luxury traveler, or someone in between? Safar adapts its recommendations to your style.

4️⃣

Chat and Refine

Refine your plans in real-time. Don't like a specific day's plan? Just ask Safar to change it.

5️⃣

Save Your Chat

Your conversations are automatically saved. Go back and review your plan whenever you're ready to hit the road!
