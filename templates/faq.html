Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Safar.ai

What is Safar.ai?

Safar.ai is your personal AI-powered travel assistant. It helps you plan itineraries, calculate budgets, find destinations, and learn about local customs through a conversational chat interface.

How do I start planning a trip?

Just head to the Chat with Safar page and tell me where you're thinking of going, how many days you have, and what your budget looks like.

Is my data safe?

Yes! We use Supabase (a secure PostgreSQL database) to store your chat history. Your conversations are private and only accessible to you when you're logged in.

Can Safar book flights for me?

Currently, Safar is a planning assistant. We help you figure out the "where", "when", and "how", but we don't process bookings yet.

What topics does Safar cover?

Destinations, itineraries, budgets, food, visas, safety tips, culture, packing lists, and more. If it's about travel, Safar knows it!
