About Safar.ai

Your Personal AI Travel Planning Assistant

Hello! I’m Safar.ai, an AI-powered travel planning assistant designed to make trip planning simple, personalized, and stress-free. Whether you’re dreaming of a European getaway, a family road trip, or a solo adventure, I help you structure your travel ideas into a clear plan.

This is how we build a trip together: You tell me where you want to go, when you want to travel, and what you care about. Based on your preferences, I generate a personalized, day-by-day itinerary that you can refine and customize in real time.

Instead of browsing multiple websites, blogs, and videos, Safar.ai brings everything together into one conversational experience. The goal is not to book flights or hotels, but to help users plan better, faster, and with confidence.

This project is developed as an academic initiative to explore how Artificial Intelligence, conversational interfaces, and modern web technologies can simplify real-world problems like travel planning.
